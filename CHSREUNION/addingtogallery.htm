Gallery Hints:
Create Albums and add photos to the Photo Gallery





  • You must register first before using these functions. See register to add to photo gallery
  • After you have registered, Go to the Our photo gallery Link.
  • Click on the photo that represents the album you would like to add to.
  • In the box on the left, click on "add items".
  • When you click on the Browse button, you will be able to browse the files on your computer. When you find the photo you would like to add,double click on it and the address will be filled in on the browse box.
  • Add a caption
  • Choose set item titles from: caption(using the dropdown box), then uncheck the next 2 boxes.
  • Make sure the Create Thumbnails Now box is checked.
  • Then click on Add Items at the bottom.
  • Your photo(s) will be added to the end of that album.

  • To create a new album:
  • After you have registered, Go to the Our photo gallery Link.
  • click on the Add Album in the box on the left.
  • Fill in the Name and Title boxes,and click on the Create button on the bottom.
  • In the box on the left, click on "add items", and follow the instructions above to add at least one photo to the album.
  • Your album will be added as the last album of the gallery.
  • At any point in the Gallery, click the large orange Gallery word on top to go back to the initial Gallery page.
  • TO PRINT THIS PAGE, click on File in the upper left corner of this page, and choose the print option.
