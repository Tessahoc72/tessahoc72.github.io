In keeping with a tradition started by the CHS class of 1956 at their 50th reunion,
we would like to celebrate our reunion by awarding a scholarship donated by our class
to help a deserving CHS student who might not be able to continue school without a financial boost.
The first award will be given to a student in the class of 2013.
We hope to make this an annual award.
