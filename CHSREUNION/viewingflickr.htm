Gallery Hints: Viewing Our Photo Sets on flickr





  • Go to "Our new photo gallery on flickr" Link on our main page.
  • Click on the set you would like to view.
  • then click on the Slideshow button near the upper right of the screen.
  • Click on Show More Info.
  • When you reach the end, press the back arrow on the upper left of your screen. Then click on "Sets" near the upper right of the flickr page.
