Gallery Hints:
Placing photos on your computer





  • The most popular methods of adding photos to a file on your computer are:
    1. download photos from your digital camera (using the USB cord and instructions provided with your camera).
    2. place photograph print on a scanner connected to your computer( your computer will require scanner-provided software in order to do this).
    3. If you do not have a scanner, you can photograph your print using your digital camera, and use step (1).
    4. If you have a film camera, you can photograph your print, and:
      • When you bring your film to be developed, along with choosing your prints, also select the 'prints on CD' option.
      • Many photo developing locations have this option(try Walgreen's, Longs, Walmart).
      • Place the CD in the CD drive on your computer. As soon as you close the drive options should appear on your system to view and save your photos to your desired file.
    5. If all else fails, you can always mail the photo(s) in a photo mailer envelope to Sue. And I will return them as soon as I scan them.
